Welcome to Dr. Steph's Portfolio. The content and design for this website is being developed as part of my experience in the StartUp Institute's New York Web Design and Product Management Track. I have dabbled in web design before arriving in New York. I am so excited to have the time to give it my full attention. Before starting this program in NYC, I had been using WordPress to create blogs, but I really want to hone in on my web design skills from the ground up. I am learning so much and it is so rewarding to see my website take shape as I play around with HTML and CSS. I have discovered, through the course of my journey so far, that I am very interested in Product Management and UX design.
